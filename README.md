# Civilizational Control Theory (CCT)
## A Programmatic Framework for Open Dissipative Multi-layer Control Systems

---

### Abstract / 摘要

#### English Version
**Abstract**— This paper introduces **Civilizational Control Theory (CCT)**, a programmatic framework that models civilizations as **open, dissipative, multi-layer control systems**. CCT conceptualizes civilizational order as a stabilized non-equilibrium process maintained through **information-energy regulation**. 

We articulate ten foundational propositions concerning:
* **Control Bandwidth & Mutual Information:** The capacity and fidelity of regulatory signaling.
* **Energy Allocation Gradients:** The physical distribution of resources to maintain order.
* **Spectral Centralization & Institutional Evolution:** The structural shifts in governance over time.
* **AI-Induced Compression of Decision Latency:** The acceleration of feedback loops through artificial intelligence.

These propositions reformulate civilizational dynamics as problems of **stability, controllability, and adaptive feedback** under entropy constraints.

---

#### 中文
**摘要**— 本文引入了**文明控制论 (Civilizational Control Theory, CCT)**。这是一个将文明建模为**开放、耗散、多层控制系统**的纲领性框架。CCT 将文明秩序概念化为一个通过**信息-能量调节**来维持的稳定非平衡过程。

我们阐述了关于以下方面的十个基础命题：
* **控制带宽与互信息：** 调节信号的容量与保真度。
* **能量分配梯度：** 维持秩序所需的资源物理分布。
* **频谱集中化与制度演进：** 治理结构随时间推移的转移。
* **AI 诱导的决策延迟压缩：** 通过人工智能实现的反馈循环加速。

这些命题将文明动力学重新表述为在熵约束下的**稳定性、可控性以及自适应反馈**问题。
